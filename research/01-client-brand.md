## Critical Gaps vs. Competitors
1. **Language barrier** — 100% Russian UI. Every competitor operates in Spanish. This limits organic search visibility and alienates second-generation or mixed households.
2. **No visual brand identity** — competitors have designed color palettes, custom fonts, photography style. BALTIKA uses a stock OpenCart theme.
3. **No social proof** — no customer testimonials, no review integration, no star ratings aggregate on homepage.
4. **No hero section** — page opens directly into a product grid with no brand story or emotional hook.
5. **No About/Story page** — competitors use brand storytelling to build trust; BALTIKA has none.
