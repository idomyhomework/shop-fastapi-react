# Competitor Analysis — Eastern European Grocery Online Spain

## Niche Definition
Online retailers selling Eastern European (Russian, Ukrainian, Polish, Bulgarian, Romanian) food products to diaspora communities in Spain. Delivery-first, Spanish territory, targeting immigrants who want the tastes of home.

## Patterns of the Top 10%

After analyzing all five competitors, these are the 5 things every winning site does that BALTIKA doesn't:

### 1. Spanish-Language Interface
Every competitor operates primarily in Spanish. This unlocks Google search traffic, serves second-generation immigrants, Spanish partners of Eastern European immigrants, and food explorers. BALTIKA's Russian-only UI is invisible to search engines for Spanish queries.

### 2. Emotional "Eat Like at Home" Narrative
Top sites lead with an emotional hook, not a product grid. Cesta Selecta: *"Las marcas más famosas de tu país para que comas como en casa."* Magnit uses warm photography and nostalgic colors. BALTIKA leads with a raw product listing.

### 3. Visible Social Proof on the Homepage
Cesta Selecta has 3 named customer testimonials with photos. Balkanica shows €27M annual revenue. Magnit shows "In stock" confidence signals. BALTIKA has zero social proof visible anywhere.

### 4. Free Shipping Prominently Communicated
Every competitor makes their free shipping threshold the first thing you see. BALTIKA has it but buries it in a separate /dostavka page. It's a conversion lever being wasted.

### 5. Modern Visual Identity
Magnit: warm cream + crimson. Cesta Selecta: forest green + cream. These aren't random — they evoke warmth, food, authenticity. BALTIKA uses electric blues that feel mismatched and cold. No brand fonts, no custom photography style.

## Recommended Design Direction (based on competitor data)

### Colors
Keep BALTIKA's navy as base but add warmth:
- Primary: `#1C3557` (deep Baltic navy — nod to the brand name)
- Warm accent: `#D4572A` (Baltic amber/terracotta — warmth, food, trust)
- Light bg: `#F8F5F0` (warm paper — premium, not clinical white)
- Text: `#2C2C2C`

### Typography
- Heading: **Playfair Display** (elegant serif — premium food brands use this)
- Body: **Inter** (clean, readable, modern)

### Photography Style
Hero images: close-up food photography — glistening herring, dark rye bread, amber honey, condensation on glass. Warm, nostalgic, appetizing.

### Animations
- Subtle scroll-fade for product cards
- Parallax on hero section
- Hover lift + shadow on product cards
- Sticky cart icon with count animation

### What to AVOID
- Electric blue color schemes (cold, untrusting for food)
- Russian-only content (invisible to search)
- Template product grids as the homepage hero
- Zero social proof
